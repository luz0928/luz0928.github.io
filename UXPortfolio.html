Paper prototype - ATM for the elderly

The aim was to design an interface for an ATM for older adults.
As you can see in the video, I invited an elderly user to test my paper prototype, I gave out the instructions to follow ( To withdraw money from the machine) and clarified that she must use it as the real thing.
When the user starts "pressing buttons", I act as the machine and change the different screens depending on the interaction.
